Your Bridge to Real Estate Investments

Bridge Capital Investment Group, LLC specializes in acquiring distressed properties from over a decade of wholesale relationships, and rehabbing the property to meet the condition of complete habitability; to ultimately result of a new family moving in and delivering the American dream.

As Founder of the company, Frank Helmand has aligned himself with some of the best General Contractors, Builders, Sub Contractors, and all facets of home repair; never compromising workmanship.
"Our model is simple; gray body, white trim, and red door, of course palms and attractive plants." We offer Investment Programs for Investors, and have a wealth of testimonials from our currently active investors.
